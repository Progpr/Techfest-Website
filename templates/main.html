Ignite is the annual flagship event of SVKM'S NMIMS Navi Mumbai, engineered by the students of School of Technology Management and Engineering (STME). Post the miraculously instalment of the successful fest, Ignite 6.0 is back in its absolute form, with events being held with more enthusiasm and ardor than ever before.


The 6th iteration of the tech fest is to catalyze innovation and foster a dynamic ecosystem where technology enthusiasts, visionaries, and creators converge to celebrate the latest advancements, explore emerging trends, and ignite collaborative endeavors.


Through immersive workshops, expert-led seminars, hands-on experiences, and vibrant networking opportunities, we aim to empower participants with the knowledge, skills, and inspiration needed to shape the future, while emphasizing ethical considerations, sustainability, and the transformative potential of technology for the betterment of society.
